Hi there! My name is Thomas and welcome to my Portfolio.

I am currently a student at the Prague University of Economics and Business, at the Faculty of Informatics and Statistics, majoring in Applied Informatics, and I am a web development enthusiast.

How I Got Started

It starts long time ago into my childhood, when I discovered computers and I knew I will be working someday with it. My brother, an experienced programmer, first ignited my passion for coding and suggested I start with Front-End-Development. I dove enthusiastically, and while it was challenging at first, discovering how websites are built was fascinating.

My university studies then introduced me to Back-End-Development, which i found equally compelling. Today, I am actively working on building my skills across both the front-end and back end.
